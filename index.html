Who I am

A determined learner

I am currently a final-year student in the Bachelor of Matematics of the University of Luxembourg.

My interests are in linear algebra and statistics, which perfectly fits with my goals to pursue a career in Machine Learning (ML). I am lucky enough to have found my passion for ML several years ago, which allowed me to progressively acquire a solid knowledge on the topic.
My learning journey has been guided by many hours of online self-teaching as well as serious consistency driven by my interest in the subject. I believe the followings are two strengths of mine:

  • Thanks to my long time passion about ML I have been exposed to many different approaches on the main concepts in the field. As a result, I have developped a good intuition and high level overview of many of those core concepts.
  • Thanks to my mathematical background I am able to quickly grasp these concepts when facing their mathematically rigorous definitions.
